Our Mission

Fast food that works
for your body,

We started Eat Right because we were tired of choosing between food that tastes good and food that's actually good for you. So we rebuilt the classics from the ground up — same indulgence, better ingredients.

Starting as a ghost kitchen in London, we're building toward a full Eat Right Diner. Every item on our menu is macro-optimised, protein-forward, and genuinely delicious.
